Food Delivery ServiceSystem

Technologies: PHP, MySQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Developed a web-based food delivery service system that enables customers to order food online and track their orders. The system supports multiple user roles to manage the delivery process efficiently.

Features:

  • User Registration and Login
  • Food Menu Management
  • Online Food Ordering
  • Order Tracking
  • Delivery Assignment and Management
  • Order Status Updates

    • User Roles:

  • Admin: Manage users, restaurants/menus, orders, and delivery personnel.
  • User (Customer): Browse menus, place orders, and track order status.
  • Delivery Personnel: View assigned orders and update delivery status.
